⚠ Important Notes — From Labels

• Glutathione — label says "empty stomach" — food reduces absorption ~30%

• ZMA — label says "Men: 3 caps before bed" — calcium in food blocks zinc completely

• BULK — start at ½ scoop — label instruction. Beta-Alanine tingling is normal

• Psyllium Husk — label serving is 9g (1 tbsp) — plan uses 5g to start

• Spirulina — refrigerate after opening — label instruction

• Omega-3 total: 2560mg/day — 2 softgels Meal 1 + 2 softgels Meal 4

• KSM-66 — takes 6–8 weeks to show full effect. Never skip days
